Great Saints

Nunar village has been a sanctuary for great souls, whose blessings and grace have been a constant source of strength for its people. Despite the challenges and void created by the unfortunate exodus of Kashmiri Pandits, we have faced these turbulent times with courage. Our community has worked tirelessly to preserve our identity, culture, and language.

Great Saints of village and around

  • Har Bab
  • Sampta Bab (Urpesh)
  • Kashi Kak (Manigam)
  • Nand Bab
  • Ramjoo Taberdar (Chinkral Mohalla)
